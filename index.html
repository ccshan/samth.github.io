Research Projects

My research focuses on the design and implementation of programming systems. I'm particularly interested in programming languages that support the evolution of software.

Typed Racket

I created and maintain Typed Racket, a statically-typed dialect of Racket that allows existing untyped Racket programs to be enriched with types.
